📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents a Sales Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze and visualize business sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into total sales, orders, profit, profit margin, product performance, and customer segments.

It helps businesses make data-driven decisions by identifying sales trends, top-performing products, and segment-wise distribution.

🎯 Key Features

Total Sales Overview – Displays overall sales performance.

Total Orders – Shows the total number of orders placed.

Total Profit – Calculates the overall profit generated.

Profit Margin – Shows profitability percentage.

Sales Trend Analysis – Line chart showing sales trend over time.

Top Products by Sales – Bar chart displaying best-selling products.

Sales by Segment – Pie chart representing segment distribution.

Product Sales Table – Detailed breakdown of product quantity and sales.
